Explore Our Departments

Madrid City University offers a variety of academic departments that cater to a broad spectrum of interests and career aspirations. Explore the departments below to learn more about what we offer:

Science Department

The Science Department provides cutting-edge education and research opportunities in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics. Our modern labs and expert faculty ensure an exceptional learning experience.

Arts Department

The Arts Department nurtures creativity and critical thinking through programs in Literature, History, Philosophy, and Fine Arts. We foster an environment that encourages artistic expression and academic exploration.

Commerce Department

The Commerce Department prepares students for the dynamic world of business with courses in Accounting, Finance, Marketing, and Management. Our strong industry connections provide real-world insights and career opportunities.

Engineering Department

The Engineering Department offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, and Computer Engineering. Our state-of-the-art facilities and industry links provide excellent hands-on training.

Law Department

The Law Department provides comprehensive programs in various branches of law, including Criminal, Civil, and International Law. We focus on developing critical thinking, legal research, and advocacy skills.

Medical Department

The Medical Department offers programs in Medicine, Nursing, and Allied Health Sciences. Our cutting-edge facilities and experienced faculty help students prepare for impactful careers in healthcare.
